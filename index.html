◆ What We Stand For

THE CODE OF
DIAMOND
STREET

We run by a simple code. No drama. No disrespect. Just real love for the machine and the culture that built us.

  • Respect every build, every budget
  • Show up, show out, represent right
  • No racing on public roads
  • Yellow & Black — ride or die colors
  • Family comes first, cars come second
  • Always help a member in need
